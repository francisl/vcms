The current serving Canadian Forces tank is the updated Leopard 1A5 designated Leopard C2 is CF service. Fifteen of these tanks were deployed to Afghanistan to provide direct fire support. ( Some sources list the deployed tanks with their extra add-on armour protection as the Leopard C2A1). Leopard-based support vehicles include: the Badger Armoured Engineering Vehicle (AEV), the Taurus Armoured Recovery Vehicle (ARV), and Beaver Armoured Vehicle-Launched Bridge (AVLB).
